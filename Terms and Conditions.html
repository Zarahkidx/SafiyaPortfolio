Eliminating LGBTQ+ Misconceptions One Idea at a Time

Recently I have been finding that people have a lot of confusions or misconceptions about the LGBTQ+ community. Little things that I realized should definitely be addressed to prevent all misunderstandings, this project here is my attempt to do this. Here I have posters and little booklets that can be easily printed out that address things that all people should be knowledgeable about when it comes to the LGBTQ+ community.
